Impressions#

A nice ride through the French countryside to test the setup for the upcoming trip to Sweden. In extreme temperatures of over 30 degrees, scrubbing 1000 m with approx. 14 kg of luggage.

The landscape is very idyllic. There is hardly anything going on. That’s good for cycling and bad for eating on the way. Nevertheless, everything went well and the setup worked.

The only thing I need to optimize a bit for Sweden is the mounting of the tent and lock. optimize a bit for Sweden so that I don’t have to put up with annoying rattling and the frame the frame.
